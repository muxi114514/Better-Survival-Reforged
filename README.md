# Better Survival Reforged

A Minecraft Forge mod for **1.20.1** that adds new weapon types, enchantments, potion systems, and cross-mod integrations to enhance your survival experience.

**Original Author**: [BlockVader](https://github.com/mujmajnkraft) (Better Survival)  
**Reforged By**: MX

---

## ⚔️ Weapons

Five new weapon categories, each with a full material tier progression (Wood → Stone → Iron → Gold → Diamond → Copper → Emerald → Crying Obsidian):

| Weapon | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 🔨 **Hammer** | Right-click to ground slam |
| 🗡️ **Dagger** | Bonus damage from behind |
| 🪓 **Battle Axe** | Can disarm opponents |
| 🔱 **Spear** | Throwable with extended reach |
| 🥊 **Nunchaku** | Combo weapon: hit faster, hit harder |

### Cross-Mod Material Tiers

- **Silver** — [Ice and Fire CE](https://github.com/iafenvoy/IceAndFire-CE) integration, +2 damage to undead
- **Dragonbone / Fire / Ice / Lightning Dragonbone** — Crafted with Ice and Fire CE materials with elemental effects
- **Fire / Ice / Lightning Dragonsteel** — Top-tier weapons with dragon element powers
- **Umbrium / Scarlite** — [Defiled Lands](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/defiled-lands) integration with bleeding/vampirism effects

## ✨ Enchantments

25+ unique enchantments including:

- **Vampirism** — Heal on hit
- **Education** — Bonus XP
- **Assassinate** — Enhanced backstab damage
- **Bash** — Stun on hit
- **Combo** — Increasing damage per consecutive hit
- **Disarm** — Knock items from enemy hands
- **Arrow Recovery / Multishot / Rapid Fire / Range** — Ranged enhancements
- **Tunneling / Diamonds Everywhere / Versatility** — Mining improvements
- **Agility / High Jump / Vitality** — Armor boosts

## 🧪 Potion System

- **Potion Cauldron** — Coat weapons with potion effects
- Custom potions: Blindness, Decay, Warp, Antiwarp, Milk, Cure, Dispel
- Full support for Splash, Lingering, and Tipped Arrow variants

## 📦 Dependencies

| Mod | Required | Purpose |
|-----|----------|---------|
| Minecraft Forge 47.3.0+ | ✅ | Mod loader |
| Minecraft 1.20.1 | ✅ | Game version |
| [Ice and Fire CE](https://github.com/iafenvoy/IceAndFire-CE) | ❌ Optional | Dragon material weapons & silver weapons |
| [JMixin](https://github.com/nicohman/JMixin) | ❌ Optional | Extended mod compatibility |

The compiled JAR will be in `build/libs/`.
